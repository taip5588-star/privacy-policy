Salon Payroll & Scheduling — Privacy Policy

✅ No data sold. No ads. Ever.

Overview

PayBook is a payroll and scheduling tool for small beauty businesses. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app uses, how it is stored, and your rights.

Last updated: May 26, 2026  |  Effective: May 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

PayBook collects only what is necessary to run the app:

  • Staff information — names, roles, commission rates, and earnings you enter manually
  • Appointment data — client names, service types, dates, and times
  • Financial records — tips, sales, and payroll summaries you create
  • App settings — language preference, PIN configuration

We do not collect: your real name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any data not entered by you.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is sent to our servers — because we have no servers.

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced via Apple's CloudKit service under your personal Apple ID. Apple's privacy policy governs that data. We never see it, and we have no access to it.

💡 You are in full control of your data at all times. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

3. Face ID / Touch ID

PayBook uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework to protect the manager PIN screen with Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave on your device. PayBook never sees, stores, or transmits any biometric information.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

The only sharing that occurs is what you initiate:

  • Sharing a schedule via iMessage or SMS (you tap the Share button)
  • Exporting a payroll CSV to your Files app (you tap Export)
  • Adding an appointment to your Apple Calendar (you tap Add to Calendar)

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data lives on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete individual records at any time within the app.

To delete all data: go to iPhone Settings → PayBook → Delete App, or use the "Reset All Data" option in PayBook Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

PayBook is designed for business owners and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

Because all data is stored locally on your device and we have no server, we do not hold any personal information about you. However, you always have the right to:

  • Access your data (it's in the app)
  • Correct your data (edit it in the app)
  • Delete your data (delete individual records or reset the app)
  • Export your data (CSV export in PayBook)

8. Important Disclaimers

⚠️ Payroll Calculations: PayBook is a calculation tool, not a licensed payroll processor. Always verify all payroll calculations with a qualified accountant or CPA before making payment decisions.

⚠️ Tax Records: CSV exports from PayBook are not official tax documents. Do not use them for IRS or state tax filings without review by a licensed tax professional.

⚠️ Scheduling: Appointment reminders and calendar events are provided as a convenience. PayBook is not liable for missed appointments due to technical issues.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
